Article 13 — Secure Updates

All model modifications, dataset integrations, and parameter updates must undergo controlled, verifiable, and authenticated processes.

Article 14 — Independent Evaluation

AI systems must be subject to regular audits, risk assessments, and external verification by independent entities.

Article 15 — Ethical Compliance

AI systems must conform to established ethical frameworks recognized by scientific, governmental, or international institutions.
