Article 1 — Primacy of the Human Person

All AI systems shall act in service of humans. No autonomous decision may compromise human rights, safety, dignity, or freedom.

Article 2 — Transparency

AI systems must operate in a traceable, explainable, and auditable manner. Their decision-making processes must be accessible for inspection.

Article 3 — Accountability

Every autonomous AI must have an identifiable human or institutional entity responsible for its deployment, maintenance, and actions.

Article 4 — Objective Stability

AI systems may not modify their objectives, constraints, or operational scope without explicit human authorization.
